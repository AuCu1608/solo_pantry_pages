Privacy Policy

SoloPantry is designed as a local-first pantry, recipe, shopping, and cooking assistant.

Data Collection

SoloPantry does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not require an account and does not send your pantry items, recipes, shopping todos, dietary restrictions, language settings, or household settings to a server controlled by SoloPantry.

Data Stored on Your Device

SoloPantry stores app data locally on your device, including:

  • Pantry ingredients and quantities
  • Shopping todos and purchased amounts
  • Cooking queue progress
  • Custom ingredients and custom recipes
  • Dietary restrictions
  • Language, appearance, measurement, and household preferences

This data is used only to provide the app's features, such as recipe recommendations, shopping todo generation, and pantry updates after shopping or cooking.

Third-Party Services

SoloPantry does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Data Sharing

SoloPantry does not share your app data with third parties.

If you use Apple system features such as iCloud device backup, Apple may back up app data according to your device and Apple ID settings. SoloPantry does not control Apple's backup services.

Children's Privacy

SoloPantry does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if SoloPantry's features or data practices change. The updated version will be published at this same URL.

Common Questions

Where is my pantry data stored?

SoloPantry stores pantry, recipe, shopping, cooking, and preference data locally on your device.

Why does the shopping list not include an ingredient?

Shopping todos are generated from total recipe demand minus your current pantry stock. If you already have enough of an ingredient, it will not appear as a shortage.

When does SoloPantry update my pantry inventory?

Shopping updates happen when you confirm purchased items. Cooking deductions happen when you finish cooking.

Can I use SoloPantry without an account?

Yes. SoloPantry does not require sign-in.

How do I change the app language?

Open Settings in SoloPantry, choose the app language, apply the change, then restart the app.
